# Intent

## Problem Statement
Our finance team's invoice handling is slow and error-prone. We want to modernise
how invoices flow through the business so users can do their work without the
current frustration. The team also wants us to investigate whether AI could help
classify or extract invoice data automatically.

## Business Context
The finance team has been growing and the manual process is reaching its limits.
There are also some compliance pressures around how supplier data is handled.

## Desired Outcomes
- Faster invoice turnaround
- Fewer errors that result in payment delays
- Better visibility into the status of invoices for the people waiting on them
- Stay compliant with supplier data handling rules
- Be able to handle a growing volume of invoices without proportional headcount

## Known Constraints
- Must comply with GDPR for supplier data
- Existing finance system (Sage) cannot be replaced — must integrate with it
